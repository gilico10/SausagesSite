Dashboard 2 - Sausage Sales Channels

Insights into Payment Methods and Sales Platforms

About This Dashboard

Dashboard 2 focuses on the distribution of sausage sales across different payment methods and sales platforms. It highlights that Commercial Credit is the leading payment method at $58,476, while Door-to-Door Sales is the top platform at $16,102. Additionally, it breaks down quantities sold by customer type, with Wholesale leading at 8,852 units. This dashboard helps optimize sales strategies by identifying the most effective channels and payment preferences.
